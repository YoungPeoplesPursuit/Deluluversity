Portraits, Commencement Pics, Delulu College Scenes...

I saved a ton of commencement pics and photos straight from official college accounts on Instagram out of my own delulu and decided to put them to use as reference pictures.

One last problem to solve

I had been working up to drawing my own senior pictures. But instead of the flower field from my dreams I wanted to tell a story about my reality. I engineered a setup that would take a picture of myself looking at myself. I was annoyed at the messy background at first but then I used the bookshelf to display some core artifacts, including my pet beaver Barker proudly sitting on the top. I did not want to render this too much and I intentionally overlayed my sketch because the solution is not done.
