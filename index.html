Why is this the University of Delulu?

Because I like being delulu and I've been directing my delulu to colleges for the last ~5 years (as of 2026), I was deciding between the University of Delulu, the Deluluversity, and U Delulu for the name of this site. I guess the University of Delulu is the most official sounding name so it graces the homepage, but I like Deluluversity the best so that is the name of the Github repo and I will be calling it the Deluluversity from now on.

Deluluversity Founding Lore

When I created this website, I was an incoming freshman who wanted to start a content creation arc that meant actually posting reels and trying to reach more people, so I needed a place to organize everything I've created.

My creations really started sophomore year when I noticed that I had abundant boredom and an abundant amount of doodles in my APUSH notebook. I decided it was time to awaken my inner artist and learn digital art. I was really trash back then (I deleted plenty of early works out of embarrassment) but I thought my art was amazing and posted everything on Instagram. Since then, my art has improved a bit and still has room to improve.

I've been looking into creating things other than art, like writing short stories and filming reels about my college experience. I have too many ideas and need more opportunities to get them out of my head.

I also want to share what I find beautiful and go delulu over (including colleges, boats, beavers, swans, bodies of water sufficiently large for boats and beavers and swans, cool artifacts I happen to find, get the vibe?) with the world and hopefully you will find something delulu too! :D
